Account and Data Deletion

To comply with data protection regulations, we provide two methods to delete your account and all associated information (stats, coins, and profile):

  1. In-App: Sign in, go to Profile > Settings and select "Delete account". The deletion is immediate and permanent.
  2. Manual Request: If you cannot access the app, please send an email to osirisstudio.games@gmail.com specifying your associated email address.

Note: Once the account is deleted, data cannot be recovered.
